Tax-free Savings Account (TFSA)

Government encourages savings and therefore initiated tax-free savings accounts that enable you to invest up to R30 000 per tax year that will be free of interest, capital gains and dividend tax.

— Retirement Planning

Someone is sitting in the shade today, because someone planted a tree a long time ago.

Saving for retirement is an on-going financial journey. Our professional guidance and advice removes the uncertainty of the unknown and ensure that you can sleep well at night. Your retirement should be something you look forward to.

Too many people start investing too late or too little at unnecessary high costs and at insufficient levels of risk. We help you invest your money at an appropriate level of risk in a cost-effective structure that provides optimal tax benefits. Let us assist you to plan ahead:

Pre-retirement Planning

It is never too early to start saving for retirement. Pre-retirement planning mostly takes the form of a lump sum investment or a continuous monthly contribution. The secret is to be committed to continuous contributions and to implement the most appropriate risk profiled investment solution.

Preservation at Resignation

This is for individuals who are retrenched or moving jobs. We assist you to retain your current retirement plan and to stay on track. The secret is to not delve into your savings.

Post-retirement Planning

If you are retired or about to retire we assist you to invest what you have accumulated up to now to provide you with a monthly income that will sustain your lifestyle, without outliving your savings.


Contact us today to meet a financial planner to ensure a worry-free retirement.


— Estate Planning

“In this world nothing can be said to be certain, except death and taxes.” – Benjamin Franklin

Taxes at death are a reality, but how much are we talking about? The taxes and administration costs at death can be significant and impact your heir’s inheritance if you do not structure it in the most effective manner.

We assist you with:

  • Setting up a will
  • Reviewing your existing will
  • Calculating the taxes and costs applicable to your estate on death
  • Reviewing the liquidity of your estate
  • Providing recommendations regarding any shortcomings

Contact us today to meet a financial planner to ensure your loved ones are looked after well.


— Risk Cover

No one can predict the future or unfortunate events such as death and disability, however, we can position you to be protected against potential losses and minimize the impact thereof.

We offer you advice on -
  • Life cover
  • Disability cover
  • Critical illness cover
  • Educational cover
  • Income protection

Additionally, we can guide you to be sure your medical aid and short term insurance are sufficient to protect your family and your lifestyle.


Contact us today to meet a financial planner for an insurance quote that will allow you to sleep well at night.
